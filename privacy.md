# NutriLens — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 22, 2026
**Data controller:** Bahri Bulut (FitnessAI)
**Contact:** bahri9954@gmail.com


This policy explains what personal data **NutriLens** ("the App") processes, why, and
what rights you have.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

### 1.1. Account information
- The App works with an **anonymous account by default** (no email/password required).
- You may optionally create a **permanent account with an email and password** to keep
  your data across devices. In that case we store your email address.

### 1.2. Health & nutrition data (sensitive data)
- **Meal photos** and the nutrition values derived from them (calories, protein, carbs,
  fat, fiber, sugar, sodium, health score).
- **Profile / goal data:** age, gender, height, weight, activity level, workout
  frequency, diet preference, allergies, target weight and motivation.
- **Weight history** and **daily meal logs**.
- With your permission, data read from your device's health platform
  (**Android Health Connect / Apple Health**): steps, active calories, distance, sleep,
  weight, heart rate. You can revoke this permission anytime in device settings.

### 1.3. AI processing data
- Your meal photos and coach chat messages are sent to **Google Gemini** (via our
  server-side proxy) to generate nutrition analysis and advice.
- AI calls are counted server-side for **daily quota** enforcement.

### 1.4. Subscription information
- If you purchase a premium subscription, purchase validation is handled by
  **RevenueCat** and the relevant app store (Google Play / App Store). We **do not see
  or store** your card/payment details; the store processes them.

### 1.5. Technical data
- Session/authentication tokens needed to run the App are stored in your device's
  secure storage (Keystore/Keychain).

Health data is **special-category personal data**; we process it only with your
**explicit consent** and to provide the service to you.

## 3. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your data. We share it only with **processors** needed to run the service:

- **Supabase** — authentication, database and file storage (hosting).
- **Google Gemini (Google)** — meal photo and text analysis (AI processing).
- **RevenueCat** + **Google Play / Apple App Store** — subscription validation and payment.
- **Google Health Connect / Apple Health** — only on your device, subject to your permission.

Some of these providers may process your data on servers outside your country, with
appropriate safeguards in place.

---

## 4. Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you **delete your account**, your
meal logs, photos, weight history, profile and identity record are **permanently
deleted** (in-app: Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete Account).
