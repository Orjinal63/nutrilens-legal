# NutriLens — Account & Data Deletion

**App:** NutriLens (by Bahri Bulut — FitnessAI)
**Contact:** bahri9954@gmail.com

This page explains how to request deletion of your **NutriLens** account and the data
associated with it.

## How to delete your account (in the app)

1. Open **NutriLens**.
2. Go to **Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete Account**.
3. Confirm. Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted from our servers.

## Delete by request (email)

If you cannot access the app, email **bahri9954@gmail.com** from your account's email
address with the subject **"Delete my account"**. We process the request within 30 days.

## What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following are **permanently removed**:

- Account and profile (email, goals, body metrics)
- Meal logs, calories and nutrition data
- Meal photos
- Weight history
- AI usage records

## What data is retained

- We do **not** retain your personal data after deletion.
- Backups containing residual copies are purged within **30 days**.
- Subscription/transaction records held by **Google Play / RevenueCat** for legal and
  accounting purposes are managed by those providers, not by us.
